About Balagi

Your Trusted Printing Partner

Balagi Printing & Design is a professional printing and branding company based in Tabokoto, West Coast Region, The Gambia.

We specialize in banner printing, flyer printing, sticker printing, paper bag production, graphic design, and printing supplies. With years of experience, we deliver quality, reliability, and exceptional service to businesses, schools, and events.
